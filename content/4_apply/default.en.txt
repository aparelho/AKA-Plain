Agder Kunstakademi is open
to prospective students who
are members of the norwegian prisonal
system. 

Prospective students
need apply through
apply@agderacademy.no

The platform / school is a collaboration of the art lectorates or professorships of the higher art education in the Netherlands. The objectives is to inform, exchange and collaborate in the field of research in the arts.
